About Me

I am a passionate MSc student in Theoretical Physics and Quantum Computing at Università degli Studi di Milano. I have been an active member of the ATLAS Collaboration. I used innovative Machine Learning techniques to improve the separation of a single top-quark produced in association with a Z boson from the background processes. I am currently writing a book on the history of Physics with professor Leonardo Gariboldi of the Università degli Studi di Milano. My ambition is to become a theoretical particle physicist and a science communicator.
